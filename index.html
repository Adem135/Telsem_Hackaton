Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who can participate?

    High school students from around the world are welcome to participate.

  • Do I need to be an experienced programmer?

    No, we welcome students of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, you'll find this hackathon valuable.

  • How do I form a team?

    Teams can be formed during the registration process or at the beginning of the hackathon. You can choose to work with friends or be matched with other participants.

  • What are the judging criteria?

    Projects will be judged based on innovation, design, functionality, and presentation.
