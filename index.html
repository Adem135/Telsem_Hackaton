About the Hackathon

The Telsem Hackathon 2024 is designed to connect high school students and empower them through programming. Participants will have the opportunity to build innovative projects, collaborate with peers, and showcase their talents. Whether you're an experienced coder or a beginner, this hackathon is for you!

Event Details

Date:

July 12, 2024

Duration:

48 hours

Theme:

Empowering Through Programming

This event will be held online, allowing students from all over the world to participate. Teams will be formed to encourage collaboration and learning.

Registration Information

To register for the Telsem Hackathon 2024, please fill out the registration form by July 5, 2024. There is no registration fee. We welcome students with all levels of programming experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

High school students from around the world are welcome to participate.

Do I need to be an experienced programmer?

No, we welcome students of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, you'll find this hackathon valuable.

How do I form a team?

Teams can be formed during the registration process or at the beginning of the hackathon. You can choose to work with friends or be matched with other participants.

What are the judging criteria?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, design, functionality, and presentation.
