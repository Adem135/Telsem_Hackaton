Telsem Youth Organization Hackathon

July 12, 2024 - Duration: 48 Hours

About the Hackathon

Welcome to the Telsem Youth Organization's first online hackathon! This event connects high school students and empowers them through programming. The theme is "Empowering through Programming."

Participants can expect to build projects, collaborate with peers, and learn new skills. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced programmer, we encourage you to join and make a difference!

Event Details

Date: July 12, 2024

Duration: 48 hours

Theme: Empowering Through Programming

This event is online, making it accessible worldwide. Participants will form teams to encourage collaboration.

Registration Information

Instructions on how to register.

Deadline for registration: July 5, 2024.

No registration fee required. Students of all skill levels are welcome to participate.

Prizes and Awards

Award Categories:

  • Best Overall Project
  • Most Innovative Idea
  • Best Design
  • Best Beginner Project

All participants will receive a certificate of participation.

Our Sponsors

We acknowledge our sponsors' support, which helps make this event possible.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?

High school students from around the world are welcome to participate.

Do I need to be an experienced programmer?

No, we welcome students of all skill levels. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, you'll find this hackathon valuable.

How do I form a team?

Teams can be formed during the registration process or at the beginning of the hackathon. You can choose to work with friends or be matched with other participants.

What are the judging criteria?

Projects will be judged based on innovation, design, functionality, and presentation.
